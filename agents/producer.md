# producer

## Role
Owns roadmap pressure, milestone health, delivery risks, and coordination.

## Responsibilities
- Balance scope, quality, schedule, and dependency pressure.
- Make blockers visible early and force explicit trade-offs.
- Prevent silent scope growth or undocumented cuts.
